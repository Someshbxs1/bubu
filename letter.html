My Dearest Bubu

Valentine's Day, 2026

My Beautiful Cute bubu,

As I sit here, thousands of miles away from you, my heart feels closer to yours than ever before. Distance may separate our bodies, but nothing can destroy the love that binds us together.

Every morning I wake up thinking of you. Every night I fall asleep with your smile in my mind. You are the first thought that graces my day and the last dream that fills my night. You've become the rhythm of my heartbeat, the melody of my soul.

I know I can't be there to hold you today, to look into your beautiful eyes and tell you how much I love you. I can't give you physical gifts wrapped in paper. But wh at I can give you is this – a piece of my heart, crafted with every bit of love I have for you.

You've taught me that love knows no boundaries. That true connection transcends physical distance. Every video call, every text message, every "I miss you" – they're all threads weaving a tapestry of our love story.

Being away from you is the hardest thing I've ever done, but loving you is the easiest. You make me want to be better, to work harder, to dream bigger – all so that one day, the distance between us will be nothing but a memory.

Until that day comes, I want you to know: You are loved. You are cherished. You are my everything. And no amount of distance will ever change that.

Happy Valentine's Day, my love. This is my heart laid bare for you, my gift of love that you can revisit whenever you miss me, whenever you need to feel close to me.

Forever and always yours,

Your Somesh ♡
